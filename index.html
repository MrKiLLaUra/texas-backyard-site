Our Story

A Texan's backyard, transplanted to Germasogeia

Texas Backyard started the way the best food does — at a festival, feeding a hungry crowd straight off a roaring grill. The burgers were huge, the timing was spot on, and the line never stopped.

Today we run the same fire from our spot on Christaki Kranou. Everything's made to order: patties pressed by hand, dogs snapped fresh, sauces mixed in-house. As one proud Texan put it after his first bite — "surprisingly delicious… definitely done his homework."
